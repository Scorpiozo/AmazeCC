# AmazeCC Color System

---

## 1. Neutral Colors

These colors define the structure of the application.

### Light Mode

Background
Surface
Elevated Surface
Border
Primary Text
Secondary Text

### Dark Mode

Background
Surface
Elevated Surface
Border
Primary Text
Secondary Text

Only design tokens may be used.

Hardcoded colors are forbidden.

---

## 2. Semantic Colors

Success

Used for:

* Safe attendance
* Completed tasks
* Positive status

Warning

Used for:

* Low attendance
* Pending actions

Danger

Used for:

* Critical attendance
* Failed operations

Info

Used for:

* Informational messages

---

## 3. Accent Themes

Users may choose accent themes.

Supported themes:

### Ocean (Default)

Blue

### Forest

Green

### Lavender

Purple

### Sunset

Orange

Only accent colors change.

Layout, spacing, and component styles remain identical.
